Seven-Layer Taco Dip

This taco dip is always a big hit at parties and family get-togethers. People gather around the platter until it's gone!

Ingredients

  • Canned Vegetables
  • Seasoning
  • Sour Cream and Cream Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Fresh Produce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Blend the beans with the seasoning mix, then spread it onto a serving platter.
  2. Mix the cream cheese and sour cream, then spread it over the beans.
  3. Top with salsa and fresh produce, then sprinkle with cheese and garnish with olives.
